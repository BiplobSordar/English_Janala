Level 3

Brisk

ব্রিস্ক

3 Points

ID: 27

MEANING

চটপটে / দ্রুত

PART OF SPEECH

Adjective

PRONUNCIATION

ব্রিস্ক

EXAMPLE SENTENCE

He took a brisk walk in the morning.

SYNONYMS

quick energetic lively active

WORD DETAILS

The word "brisk" describes something done in an active, quick, or energetic way. It often refers to movement or activity that is invigorating and efficient.
